Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SwapBox

What if I miss pickup day? ‚ñº

Don't worry! We send you a text reminder 1-2 days before pickup. If you miss it, simply contact us and we'll reschedule your pickup for the next available day at no extra charge.

What type of litter is used? ‚ñº

We use premium clumping, lightly scented litter that's veterinarian-approved and safe for all cats. It's dust-free, highly absorbent, and excellent at controlling odors.

How big are the SwapBoxes? ‚ñº

Each SwapBox is a 40-gallon Sterilite wheeled industrial tote. It's large enough to hold a full week of used litter boxes while remaining easy to move thanks to the sturdy wheels.

What if I have multiple cats? ‚ñº

Simply add more SwapBoxes to your plan! Each additional SwapBox includes 7 more litter boxes and bags of litter. Most customers with 2-3 cats choose our Multi-Cat plan with 2 SwapBoxes.

Where do I leave the bin? ‚ñº

You can leave your SwapBox anywhere outside your house that's easily accessible‚Äîyour porch, driveway, garage entrance, or side of the house. We'll find it and swap it out!

What happens to the used litter boxes? ‚ñº

We dispose of all used litter and boxes in an eco-friendly manner. The SwapBox container itself is sanitized, cleaned, and reused to minimize environmental impact.

Can I cancel anytime? ‚ñº

Yes! There are no long-term contracts. You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. We make it easy to manage your account online.

How do I use one box per day? ‚ñº

Each morning (or your preferred time), simply set out a fresh pre-filled litter box for your cat. At the end of the day or next morning, close the lid on the used box and place it in your SwapBox. It's that simple!
